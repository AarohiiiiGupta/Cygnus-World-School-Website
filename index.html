About Us

Cygnus World School Logo

Cygnus World School is a new-age, co-educational school with modern academic, co-curricular and sports facilities. Spread across 7.3 acres, the school's green campus is modelled like an educational village, where-in each of the three schools. Life at Cygnus is vibrant, exhilarating and dedicated to the joy of learning. We are aware that learning is most rewarding in a community that is lively, co-operative, critical and fundamentally curious. To be such a community of students, teachers, administrators and parents is our consistent endeavour. Both the campus and curriculum have been meticulously designed to aid in the achievement of our goals.

Principal and Director:


Kanchan Joshi

Kanchan Joshi

Kanchan Joshi, the Principal of Cygnus World School is a Post Graduate in Chemistry with specialisation in Organic Chemistry. Immediately after completing her PG she joined as a teacher of Chemistry at one of the most reputed schools of Lucknow. She taught Science in the Middle School and Biology and Chemistry in Senior Secondary. Her commitment towards education motivated her pursue her professional degree of Bachelor in Education which she completed as a regular student from Lucknow University.


Bhavani Raghuraman

Bhavani Raghuraman

Mrs. Bhavani Raghuraman, one of the most respected names in the field of academia, has always been a passionate educationist and facilitator. Her long held faith in education's ability to amplify both individual and societal intellect and hence drive world progress, has led her on her journey of 34 years in education, out of which 21 have seen her as the principal of various reputed institutes.
